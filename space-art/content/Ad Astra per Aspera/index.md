In the pursuit of the exploration of our universe, there have been close calls, losses and challenges that we have faced and had to overcome. Almost every mission, human or unmanned that has been launched has encountered some sort of issue that must be overcome in order to complete the mission. Whether that be minor issues that may constitute minor changes to mission operations or the mission plan, or more serious issues that significantly alter the outcome or goals of the mission - Apollo 13, Boeing Starliner Crew Flight test being some examples of these types of missions. 

Some missions however, have been lost in the pursuit of exploration. These tragic losses are ingrained into our collective consciousness and are even more deeply ingrained into our space programs culture. We promise to never forget those who have made the ultimate sacrifice and will carry their memories with us.
